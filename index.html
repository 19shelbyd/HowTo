How To Do A Leap

Step 1

The first part of doing a leap is stretching. The legs are the main part.
The hamstrings need to be completely stretched out so a muscle is not pulled. The calves and hips also need to be warmed up.

Step 2

Once the legs are warmed up the next part is jumping. A leap is where the legs separte into a split position in the air. The body is glided through the air.
The legs need to be totally straight and the ankles and toes need to be stretched.
The front leg will have a kicking action to get it into the air, and the back leg pushes off the ground and pulls back to make a split postion in the air.

Step 3

The arms also have something to do. One arm will go in front of the face using energy to pull it out. The other arm goes out to the side.
The head is also lifted, and it is looking over the front arm. The shoulders need to be pushed dowm, and the neck is long.
